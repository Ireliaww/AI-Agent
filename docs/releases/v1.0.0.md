### Components
- **PDF Processing**: PyMuPDF (fitz) for academic paper parsing
- **Vector Database**: ChromaDB with cosine similarity search
- **Embeddings**: Google Gemini text-embedding-004 (768D vectors)
- **LLM**: Gemini 2.5 Pro for generation, Flash for routing
- **Tokenization**: tiktoken (cl100k_base)
